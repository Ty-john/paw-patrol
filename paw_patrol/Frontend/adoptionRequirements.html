Adoption Requirements

Here's what we need from you to ensure the best match for our pets

Personal Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Current contact information (phone & email)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or lease)
  • Employment verification (recent pay stub)

Household Requirements

  • Stable, safe living environment
  • Secure yard or appropriate space for the pet
  • If renting, landlord approval for pet ownership
  • Landlord contact information (for renters)
  • Plans for pet care during work/travel

Financial Requirements

  • Ability to afford adoption fees
  • Stable income to support pet care expenses
  • Budget for food, supplies, and medical care
  • Access to veterinary services
  • Emergency fund for unexpected medical costs

Pet Care Experience

  • Prior pet ownership experience (preferred but not required)
  • References from previous veterinarians
  • Understanding of pet behavior and training
  • Willingness to attend training sessions if needed
  • Commitment to lifelong pet care

Family & Lifestyle

  • Ages of children in the household
  • Current pets and their compatibility
  • Work schedule and time available for the pet
  • Activity level matching the pet's needs
  • Plans for the pet's future (10+ years)

Legal & Commitment

  • Sign adoption agreement and contract
  • Commitment to spay/neuter if applicable
  • Commitment to annual veterinary checkups
  • No history of animal abuse or neglect
  • Agreement to return pet if adoption doesn't work out

Ready to Adopt?

If you meet these requirements, you're ready to start the adoption process. We're excited to help you find your perfect pet!
